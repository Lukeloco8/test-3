METAL MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man




Metal Man is one of the standout Robot Masters in Mega Man 2, known for his sleek design and the rapid‑fire precision of his Metal Blades. His factory stage captures his mechanical personality perfectly: shifting conveyor belts, churning gears, and glowing hazard lights create a sense of nonstop motion that keeps players on their toes as they fight their way toward him.




Defeating Metal Man rewards Mega Man with the famously overpowered Metal Blade, a weapon that fires in eight directions and barely uses any energy. The irony that Metal Man himself is extremely weak to his own weapon has become a long‑running fan joke. With his stylish look, fast fight, and unforgettable stage, he remains one of the most iconic parts of Mega Man 2.




Metal Man is easiest to beat if you stay on the ground and bait his jumps—he reacts to your movement more than he attacks at random. Fight on the side of the conveyor belt that moves against you for better control, and fire when he lands. If you have the Quick Boomerang, it works well early on, but in the rematch his own Metal Blade destroys him instantly.
